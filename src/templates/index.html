How Does my Chat Bot Work

Import Knowledge Base

Knowledge base is the training data. For example, the chatbot needs to know what an apple is to be able to answer questions including the word "apple."

Process the Data

Data is raw and messy, so we need to convert the incoming data to upper or lower case to help it understand. This helps avoid misrepresentation and misinterpretation of the same word in different cases.

Text Case Handling

Same as above, but it's put as a major step as well because it's that vital.

Tokenization

Process of converting a sentence into an individual collection of words.

Stemming

Stemming is the process of finding similarities between words with the same root words. This allows you to ignore words like "jump," "jumps," "jumping," "jumped" since they all have the root word "jump."

Bag of Words (BOW)

The Bag of Words is like a bag of all the words in a language. Generate words into numbers by generating vector embeddings. Since we have tokenized and stemmed the original sentence, the order of the words does not matter, so just take the word and use the Bag of Words to find the corresponding BOW vector for it. To stick them together, you use the matrix multiplication dot operation.

One Hot Encoding

Process by which categorical variables are converted into a form that ML algorithms use. Even though the whole sentence is not important anymore, the individual words are still important.
